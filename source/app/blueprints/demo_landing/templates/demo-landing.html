Please read carefully the following

Do not upload any illegal or confidential materials

Do not blindly download and open files from other users

Respect a responsible disclosure of 30 days

If you notice anything suspicious, please contact us immediately. We're monitoring but we might miss some.

If you find a vulnerability, please contact us before going public as it may impact systems in production. In other words, please respect a responsible disclosure of 30 days. We will patch and then publish the vulnerability. It will have your name except if you don't want to. You can report anything you find at contact@dfir-iris.org

The scope of the security test is limited to the Web Application IRIS hosted on d1.beta.dfir-iris.org. Subdomains, scanning the IP, BF the SSH, and other flavors are out of scope.
